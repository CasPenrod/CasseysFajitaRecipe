Black Bean Fajitas

Great make ahead freezer meal

| Prep Time: 8 Minutes | Serves: 4 People |



Quantity Ingredient
3 Bell Peppers
1 Can (14.5 oz) Black beans(drained and rinsed)
1 Large Onion
1 Tbsp Honey
1 Tbsp Apple cider Vinegar
2 Tbsp Chili Powder
2 tsp Ground Cumin
1 tsp Paprika
1 tsp Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
To Taste Salt and Pepper
1/2 Cup Mild Guac
1/2 Cup Sour Cream
1/2 Cup Shredded Colby Jack Cheese
4 Tortillas

Freezer Meal Preperation

  1. Chop onion to desired size
  2. Drain and rinse black beans
  3. Add Black beans and onion to large freezer safe bag
  4. Mix spices together with the black beans and onion
  5. Flatten and store in the freezer

Cooking Directions

  1. Defrost in fridge over night
  2. Place in slow cooker and cook on low for 8 hours
  3. Serve on torillas and top with Guac, Sour Cream, and Cheese
